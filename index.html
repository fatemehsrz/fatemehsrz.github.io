Fatemeh Salehi Rizi

Hi! I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Dr. Alan Yuille. I am a member of Computational Cognition, Vision, and Learning.

I obtained B.S. in Computer Science at Fudan University in 2018. I also spent time at Google Research, Waymo, ByteDance, NTU, and TuSimple.
